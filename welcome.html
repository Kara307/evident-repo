Welcome

We are Michael Grossklaus and Kara Lyon and we make trial graphics. For more than 20 years, litigators from top law firms across the country have relied on us to transform complex fact patterns into clear, easy-to-understand demonstratives for use in jury exercises, trials, and arbitrations. Our charts, graphs, illustrations, call-outs, testimony slides, animations, maps, timelines, scientific tutorials, and financial models have been instrumental in helping our clients reach favorable outcomes at trial. In our combined 40+ years of experience we’ve seen dramatic changes in technology, but the principles of good trial graphics remain the same. We’ve learned what works and what doesn’t, and we put this website together to share our best information with you.

Mike & Kara
